ANALYSIS OF VARIANT A RESULTS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Pitch Error Reduction (-5.6pp):
  Before: "Examples: '#5', 'b3'. A '1' with dot ABOVE may render as 'i'/'í'"
  After:  "CRITICAL RULE: When you see digit '1' on the page → output {\"p\":\"1\"}"
  Impact: The explicit rule reduced transposition errors. The VLM now better understands
          that jianpu digits are absolute, not relative to key.

Duration Error Reduction (-11.1pp):
  Before: "A '-' after a note extends duration. '5 -' = half..."
  After:  "Baseline (no underline) = quarter 'q'
           ONE underline below = eighth 'e'
           TWO underlines = sixteenth 's'
           Duration extension: '5 -' = half..."
  Impact: Separating duration notation from extension marks reduced confusion.
          The error rate dropped from 63.9% to 52.8%, suggesting better underline recognition.

Octave Error Slight Improvement (-2.8pp):
  Before: "dot(s) ABOVE = +1 (or +2 for two dots), BELOW = -1 (or -2), none = 0"
  After:  "Dot ABOVE digit → octave +1 (high)
           Dot BELOW digit → octave -1 (low)
           NO dot/marker → octave 0 (middle)"
  Impact: Modest improvement. Octave markers remain challenging because they are
          visually small and often lost in preprocessing. Image resolution increase
          might be needed for larger gains.

REMAINING ISSUES (Opportunities for Variant B & C)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Pitch (31 errors, 86.1% still):
  - Digits still frequently misread (e.g., '1' as '3', '5' as '2')
  - Systematic pattern less pronounced than before, suggesting random confusions now
  - Could benefit from: Visual digit comparison examples, clearer digit shapes

Duration (19 errors, 52.8% still):
  - Predominantly duration extension marks being misread ('q' vs 'h', 'e' vs 's')
  - Some notes getting extra dots incorrectly (dots=1 when should be 0)
  - Could benefit from: ASCII art showing underline patterns, separation of dots from dashes

Octave (13 errors, 36.1%):
  - High octave notes frequently losing their markers (M3 N3, M4 N0, etc.)
  - Low octave (oct=-1) being read as high octave ('i' character confusion)
  - Could benefit from: Image preprocessing (increase _VLM_MAX_DIM to 1600-1800),
    explicit high/low octave examples
